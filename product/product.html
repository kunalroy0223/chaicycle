The Magic of Upcycled Tea Seeds

At ChaiCycle, we believe in circular beauty and nutrition. Every bottle of our Essential Tea Seed Oil starts with the byproduct of local tea estates—seeds that are often overlooked. Through our gentle cold-pressing method, we extract a versatile oil that boasts a smoke point of 485°F, making it superior to olive oil for cooking.

Its molecular structure is remarkably similar to the skin's natural sebum, allowing it to penetrate deeply without leaving a greasy residue. Truly a "miracle oil" from the heart of the tea gardens.
